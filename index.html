WHO WE ARE

In 1869, inspired by a vision of a fraternity grounded in love, honor, and truth, James Frank Hopkins and a group of courageous men founded Sigma Nu at Virginia Military Institute. They sought to create a brotherhood that would provide not only friendship but also support for personal and moral growth. With a strong commitment to academic excellence and leadership, Sigma Nu quickly expanded its reach, establishing chapters across the nation and becoming the first national fraternity with a presence in Canada. Today, Sigma Nu continues to thrive as a testament to its founders' dedication, fostering the development of its members and making a positive impact on campuses and communities worldwide.

OUR CREED

BROTHERHOOD

Brotherhood is the strong connection that members of our fraternity, Sigma Nu, establish with their fellow brothers. It is the shared experiences, camaraderie, and heartfelt conversations that contribute to making the years spent in college and as part of Sigma Nu some of the most memorable in a brother's life. From experiencing constant support and friendship to having a space where one can authentically express themselves, Sigma Nu cultivates a profound sense of belonging for all its members. This sense of belonging becomes evident as our brothers actively participate in a variety of campus activities—whether it's sharing meals, studying together, working out, or simply spending time in each other's company. This collective spirit defines Sigma Nu and shapes it into a distinctive and special fraternity!

BUGGY

Sigma Nu has participated in buggy since the first race in 1920, proudly contributing to the rich legacy of this exhilarating Carnegie Mellon University tradition. As one of the pioneering fraternities to take part in the early years of Buggy, Sigma Nu's dedication to the sport underscores their commitment to teamwork, innovation, and campus camaraderie. With a history spanning decades, their continued involvement in the buggy races showcases their enduring spirit and the enduring bonds forged through friendly competition and shared experiences on the racetrack.

RECRUITMENT

Sigma Nu offers much more than just a typical fraternity experience; it's a crew of supportive brothers who constantly inspire one another to excel. Our welcoming house buzzes with genuine camaraderie, inside jokes that'll have you cracking up, and memories you'll treasure for life. Whether it's late-night runs to Pie Express, painting the fence at 3 am, or bonding over games of Smash Bros in the Zoo, these are the kind of traditions that make being a Sigma Nu brother awesome. Join us during recruitment and become part of the fun!

SERVICE

Sigma Nu's enduring commitment to service has been an integral part of our fraternity's identity from its very beginning. Throughout the years, we have upheld a proud tradition of making a positive impact on both our campus community and beyond. Our partnership with St. Jude Children's Research Hospital stands as a testament to our dedication to philanthropy and giving back. We support St. Jude's mission to advance research and provide compassionate care to children and families facing life-threatening illnesses though various fundraising events throughout the year such as Casignu, our annual casino night. Casignu is more than a good time—it's a testament to our commitment to championing causes that matter.

LETTER FROM OUR PRESIDENT

Welcome to the Delta Sigma Chapter of Sigma Nu at Carnegie Mellon University! Established in 1916, our chapter holds a significant place in the history of Sigma Nu on this campus. As one of the founding groups for buggy in 1920, we have a proud tradition of participating in this exciting event, which we continue to do with great enthusiasm to this day.

At Delta Sigma, we are dedicated to upholding the values of brotherhood, scholarship, leadership, and personal growth that define Sigma Nu. Our brotherhood is built on strong bonds, mutual support, and shared experiences that create lifelong friendships and a sense of belonging within the fraternity.

We take great pride in being leaders both academically and socially. Our members are involved in a diverse range of activities on campus, from excelling in their studies to holding leadership positions in various student organizations. But it's not just about individual achievements; we believe in giving back to the community and making a positive impact. That's why our philanthropic events are dedicated to supporting St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, a cause we are passionate about and committed to.

Through our dedication to excellence and our close-knit brotherhood, we strive to foster an environment where members can grow personally and professionally. The experiences and values instilled during our time at Sigma Nu serve as a solid foundation for success in all aspects of life.

Being a brother of the Delta Sigma Chapter has been a transformative experience for me personally. The support and camaraderie I've found here have made my college years truly unforgettable. I am honored to be a part of this fraternity and to contribute to its legacy.

Fraternally,

Liam McDowell
